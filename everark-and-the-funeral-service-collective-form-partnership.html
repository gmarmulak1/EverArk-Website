EverArk and The Funeral Service Collective Form Partnership

EverArk and The Funeral Service Collective look to bring innovation to the cemetery and funeral industry.

Denver, CO – Today, EverArk and the Funeral Service Collective, both agree to a formal partnership.  Both teams are looking to bring innovation and new ideas to the death care industry as it still lags behind in adopting technology to improve operations.  The Funeral Collective will lead the sales charge of bringing the EverArk cemetery management and digital legacy software to market.  They will be joining and supporting EverArk at the ICCFA Las Vegas conference in March, where EverArk will be officially launching its software.
